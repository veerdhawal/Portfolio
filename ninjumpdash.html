Experience

On Ninjump Dash, I joined the team near its global launch and was tasked with creating 2 new levels for post launch update. Before I began designing the levels, I set certain guidelines which I followed once I began creating the levels. I envisioned the levels set in the exotic lands of Himalayan mountains which also gave the game something unique in terms of level art not found in other previous levels. This allowed the levels to stand out in the post launch update. I named the levels after the mythical lands of Shambhala and Shangri-la.

 

In terms of gameplay,  my objective was to bring some unique platforming gameplay into the level not present in the previous levels. For this I designed some unique LD-patterns for platforming using the jump-pad as well as modifying the platforming template blocks.
