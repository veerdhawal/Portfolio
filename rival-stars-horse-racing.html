Experience

My primary responsibilities involved creating live-ops levers, evaluating the current features to optimize their ARPDAU and retention contribution, balancing the game for increased engagement and create future opportunities to grow the game. In live-ops, I was responsible for creating different live-events, scheduling them, evaluating their performance and optimizing them. My other live-ops responsibilities involved creating limited time offers to increase daily revenue.

 

I was also responsible for evaluating the current features by analyzing the current metrics for individual features, understanding player behavior and then drafting a proposal to either optimize the feature or improving them with new additions. I use tableau report for this in my day to day work. I worked closely with the analytics team to improve the data tracking of current as well as new features to gain valuable actionable insight.

 

I worked closely with the Lead designer, other game designer and the UX designer to create and discuss feature proposals, based on the findings and prepare an execution plan which would be then presented to the Product Owner to prioritize them in the milestone and upcoming sprints.
