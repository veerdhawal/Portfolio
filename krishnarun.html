Experience

I was responsible for designing and implementing all the levels into the game. My first job was to get the pipeline set for implementing the level gameplay and the level art, optimizing the workflow between level artist, programmer and me. We took the modular approach for the level art so that we could offload the process of composing the level in the editor from the artist and allowed me to quickly get the level in the game. To do this I defined the modular art blocks that were required which were used as template to create the art assets by the artist. This also allowed us to work in parallel and reduced the overall timeline of the project. Another significant change we did once I joined the team was to go 3D. The reason for this was reduce build size and optimization. Once the pipleline was set, I designed the levels and level bosses.

 

Since the game is based on the character of Lord Krishna from Hindu Mythology and his encounters with various enemies of his time, we had a strong background narrative and character development already in place for the bosses. The boss fight were designed keeping their personality in mind and their key story elements.
