Description

Battle Champions is my take on the two huge genres, Match-3 RPG and Turn-Based Squad RPG, that have been in the grossing games on the mobile platform. Having played Empires and Puzzles and Idle heroes for a long time (1+ years), I felt there was a huge potential of combining the addictive match-3 mechanics of Empires and Puzzles and the combat gameplay depth of Idle Heroes into a single game. The match-3 mechanics gave the hook needed for new casual players to get into the midcore RPG genre while the deep combat gameplay gave the complexity needed to build a deep character progression system to create a long lasting meta-game.
