Project Details

Studio: Ubisoft, India

Platform: Nintendo 3DS

Genre: Combat Car Racer

Role: Level Designer

Timeline: January 2011 - August 2011

Gameplay

Key Responsibilities

  • Layout Design
  • Gameplay Ingredient Distribution
  • Gameplay Integration
  • Grayboxing

Tools Used

Photoshop: Level layout design

3D Studio Max: Level blockout

Ubisoft Proprietary Game Engine: Level integration, gameplay ingredient placement and playtesting

Experience

This was my first project as a level designer and I joined the project as the second level designer in production phase. The game was being ported to Nintendo 3DS from its  Nintendo Wii conole counterpart.  My responsibility was to shortlist the layout of 3 out of 12 levels from the original game, modify it, adapt it for the handheld console we were developing for. Also, I also designed two completely new arenas which had different gameplay not present in the original title.

While working on the project, I learned how to work within the constraints of the platform while also striking a balance between the capabilities of the platform and the required gameplay. It was a key experience for me to work with a team on a project which helped me a lot in all my future projects.
