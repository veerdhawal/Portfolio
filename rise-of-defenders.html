Experience

Rise of Defenders was my second project at Cympl Studio. I took on the initiative when the team was looking for its next game and came up with the proposal for it. I chose this game based on the current market trend at that time and how well it fit with the vision of the studio. To bring something new to a genre filled with many competitors, I introduced the concept of elemental powers and skill to the tower. This added an extra layer of depth to the game by bringing deeper synergy between the towers and the enemies. I also added a new dynamic element to the narrative of the game. The world map changed as the player completed levels, restoring the war-torn land to prosperity. This was reflected in the art of the world map after every level.

 

Working on Rise of Defenders was a great experience for my growth as I was able to spearhead an entire project from its inception. I not only oversaw the production of the game but also designed every single details of the game while setting up the pipeline and workflow. I discussed and coordinated with the team members to define the vision of the game in detail. Throughout its development, I took on multiple role of designing, managing the team and co-art directing the game with the art director.
