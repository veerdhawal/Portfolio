Description

Elemental was my hobby project I started because the mechanics of matching the elements by connecting them together instead of swapping intrigued me at that time. I developed the game during my early days of becoming Level Designer and it had no commercial goal. The main art of the elemental orbs was done by my colleague and friend and the rest of the art was done by me. I playtested the game with few of my friends and they had fun playing it.
