Experience

Maskgun was my first  experience in designing a F2P game for mobile. I was involved in its conception phase and responsible for designing its game loops and its meta game features. During its designing, my understanding of the F2P model was significantly improved. These learnings became a key part of the game as I integrated retention and monetization features within the game loops. Maskgun is build for long-term gameplay with a strong progression system.

 

I was involved in supporting the game post soft-launch with major feature updates, analyzing data and system balancing. We shipped major post soft launch features like crafting and clans during my involvement with the project. Maskgun was developed by a very small team of 8, hence I had the good opportunity of taking on the role of Product Manager as well along with my designing ones. This helped broadening my skill set further and also in understanding the life cycle of a F2P game.
